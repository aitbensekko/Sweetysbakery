When flour and water mix, enzymes break down starch into simple sugars. <a href="/yeast-bread-troubleshooting" style="color: #E65A88; text-decoration: underline;">Wild yeast</a> consumes these sugars, producing carbon dioxide (bubbles) and alcohol, which leaven the bread.
