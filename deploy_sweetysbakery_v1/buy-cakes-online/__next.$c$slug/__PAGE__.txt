        <h3>How Does Shipping Work?</h3>
        <p>Shipping a perishable, fragile item like a cake seems impossible, but the logistics are fascinating.
        <br><strong>1. Flash Freezing:</strong> Most bakeries bake the cake fresh and then flash-freeze it immediately. This locks in the moisture.
        <br><strong>2. Packaging:</strong> The cake is placed in a custom-molded Styrofoam cooler or insulated box.
        <br><strong>3. Dry Ice:</strong> The cooler is packed with dry ice (solid carbon dioxide) which keeps the cake frozen for 24-48 hours without melting into water like regular ice.
        <br><strong>4. Arrival:</strong> When the cake arrives, you simply let it thaw on the counter for a few hours. It tastes just as fresh as the day it was baked.</p>
